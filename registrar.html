Vision

The Office of the Registrar envisions a future where academic processes are seamless, technology-driven, and student-centered.

Mission

The Office of the Registrar aims to support and enhance the academic experience of students, faculty, and staff by maintaining accurate academic records, facilitating registration processes, and providing responsive and efficient service in accordance with institutional policies and regulations. The office is committed to promoting student success, fostering a culture of academic integrity, and contributing to the overall mission, vision and goals of the City College of Davao.

Core Values

Academic Integrity

Genuine Service

Innovative Academic Operations

Law-Abiding Office

Accurate Records Management

  4. To equip the Registrar personnel with enough trainings and exposures
